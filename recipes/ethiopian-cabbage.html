Ethiopian Cabbage Dish

Description

This traditional dish is a vegan ethiopian side normally served with injera. It includes cabbage, potatoes, and carrots which are sauted in spices. It's simple and tasty!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 4 medium carrots, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 head cabbage, scredded
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ¼ tsp ground tumeric
  • 5 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add carrots and onion; cook and stir until beginning to soften, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in cabbage, salt, pepper, cumin, and turmeric; cook for 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. Add potatoes; reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and cook until potatoes are soft, about 20 minutes.
